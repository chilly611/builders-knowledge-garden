01 · Framework

The three-layer framework

Mysterious and palatable don't meet in the middle. They stack. A dreamer scrolling past sees the clean foreground. A pro who lingers discovers the engraved heritage underneath. At peak moments, every user gets full revelation.

Layer 01 · always present

Heritage ground

Under everything. Engraved instruments, blueprint grids, catalog typography, graphite-on-trace. Slightly faded. Feels like the app arrived on top of something drawn by hand for a hundred years.

Layer 02 · what users touch

Functional foreground

Restrained, modern, high-legibility. Clean type, generous whitespace, simplified emblems at silhouette fidelity. Never gatekeeps the dreamer; never condescends to the pro.

Back Record estimate
Layer 03 · earned moments

Moment punctuation

Where the weird earns its keep. Compass first load. Project close-out. A workflow completing. Blueprint lines draw themselves. The tool-tree grows a branch. Revelation, not decoration.

05 · Textures

Background textures · cultural memory required

Backgrounds must carry cultural memory. Pure geometric texture codes sci-fi/NFT, not blueprint-warm. Acceptable texture moves carry crumbs: paper tooth, coffee rings, pencil smudges, registration marks.

08 · Motion

Animation register · motion earns its cost

Stillness is the default. Motion appears at four moments only. Stroke-draw and graphite-dust dissolve. No bouncy easing — hand-drawn cubic-bezier(.4,.02,.2,1). Never auto-loop in production.

What v1 commits to

  1. Three layers — heritage ground, functional foreground, moment punctuation. Not one flat aesthetic.
  2. Blueprint navy + trace paper + graphite + one brass accent. No other colors without a fight.
  3. Tool-tree as the logomark — silhouette-first, dissolving into blueprint linework at hero scale.
  4. Closed motif set of six. Additions only by trade-in.
  5. Cultural-memory texture rule. No pure geometric backgrounds.
  6. Stillness is default. Motion only at four specific moments.
