## Opening — Knowledge Gardens (90 seconds)

**The pitch in one sentence**

- *Knowledge Gardens is a platform for AI-augmented domain expertise — where deep knowledge in a field becomes living, browsable, and operational instead of buried in PDFs and tribal memory.*

**Two products, one platform**

- **Builder's Knowledge Garden** — for the construction industry. The "AI COO" that knows how to run a complex build from dream to delivery.
- **Orchids** — for botanical intelligence. 400 species, 69 genera, deep field-guide knowledge made navigable.
- *Same platform underneath. Different domains expressed.*

**Why this matters now**

- Trades and field expertise are bleeding institutional knowledge — retirements, specialization gaps, fewer apprentices.
- Generic AI ("ask ChatGPT") doesn't have the trade-specific judgment a 30-year framer has.
- Knowledge Gardens makes that judgment a queryable, growing asset of the business — not a person who walks out the door.

## The Marin Farmhouse Demo (3–5 minutes)

URL it lands on: `builders.theknowledgegardens.com/projects/proj-chen-farmhouse` (or the canonical UUID variant)

**What the doctor sees first**

- Top chrome: budget ribbon ($312K spent / $1.65M total) and the 7-stage journey
- *"This is the cockpit a contractor sees every day. Always-on visibility into where the money is and where the build is."*

**Walk the 7 stages — talking points for each:**

### 1. Size Up — 100% complete
- *"Where every build starts. What is it? Where? How big? Ballpark budget?"*
- *"In Marin's case: 4 bed, 3 bath, 4,000 sqft, ~$1.65M envelope."*
- *"Notice it's done. We're past it."*

### 2. Lock — 100% complete  
- *"Scope agreement signed by the client. Budget baseline locked. E-signature."*
- *"This is the moment a handshake becomes a contract. We make that one step, not three weeks of back-and-forth."*

### 3. Plan — 85% complete (this is the "rich" stage)
- Click into Plan if there's time.
- *"Phases laid out. Codes pulled — see the Title 24 and CALGreen requirements right next to the phase they apply to. Timeline 37 weeks. Insight: running framing + rough trades concurrently saves 3 weeks and ~$28,500 in overhead."*
- *"That insight is the platform's value. It's not a calendar — it's a calendar that knows construction."*

### 4. Build — 42% complete (where we are now)
- *"Framing's done. MEP rough-in in progress."*
- *"$312K spent, $186K committed, $1.15M still in the bank."*
- *"This is the stage where most software loses contractors. We keep them in by making it the easiest screen, not the most cluttered."*

### 5. Adapt — 0%, coming up
- *"Change orders. Schedule shifts. Pay applications. Every build has them. Most contractors lose money here because the paperwork is brutal."*
- *"We make this the place where changes become billable, not where they become unpaid."*

### 6. Collect — 0%, future
- *"Draws, final inspections, lien releases. The closeout phase."*

### 7. Reflect — 0%, future
- *"Project memory. What went well, what didn't, what to do differently next time. This is where the Knowledge Garden grows."*
- *"Each project the contractor ships, the platform learns more. Their next quote is better because their last build taught it something."*

## The Close — the Contractor Partner Program (90 seconds)

- *"We're not launching to the general market yet. We're picking ~50 contractors as design partners — brand ambassadors."*
- **The offer:** Lower pricing on the platform in perpetuity, in exchange for:
  - Shaping what we ship next (their workflows drive our roadmap)
  - Allowing us to use their build data (anonymized) to improve the AI
  - Being publicly named as a launch partner when we open broadly
- *"It's a partnership, not a sale. We grow with them, they grow with us."*
- *"For a contractor running $5M–$20M a year of builds, this saves them 6–10 hours a week and meaningfully reduces change-order leakage. The payback isn't even close."*

**Where the doctor comes in (if it's that kind of conversation)**

- *"I'm raising / advising / introducing — [whatever the doctor's role is]."*
- *"If you know a contractor who runs custom residential at this scale and would be a great design partner, that's the most valuable introduction you can make right now."*

## If something breaks during the demo

- **Page won't load** — refresh once. If still broken, switch to the screenshots backup (see brief).
- **Numbers look wrong** ($NaN, $0, blank) — flag it as "we're literally shipping fixes this morning, this is the working version" and move on.
- **A button does nothing** — go to the browser URL bar, type the next stage directly: `builders.theknowledgegardens.com/killerapp/stages/<stage-name>`
- **The doctor asks a technical question you can't answer** — *"Good question, let me get you a real answer after this — Chilly's the right person for that one."* Don't bluff.
