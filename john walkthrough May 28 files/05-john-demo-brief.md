## Key talking points

- **Knowledge Gardens** is the umbrella platform. Two products: Builder's Knowledge Garden (construction) and Orchids (botanical intelligence). Same platform underneath.
- **Builder's Knowledge Garden** is the "AI COO" for construction. Helps contractors run a build from dream to delivery, 7 stages.
- **The contractor partner program:** ~50 design partners. Lower pricing in perpetuity in exchange for shaping the platform and being launch partners.
- **Target contractor:** running $5M–$20M/year of custom residential builds. Saves them 6–10 hr/week and cuts change-order leakage.

## Don't say

- *"This is just an MVP"* → Say "Minimum Lovable Product" or "early version." We use **MLP**, never MVP.
- *"It might be buggy"* → Say "We're literally shipping fixes this morning — this is the working build."
- *"I'm not sure"* → Say "Good question, let me get you a real answer."

## Do say

- *"This is what we'll ship to contractors in days."*
- *"The platform learns from every project — that's what makes it different."*
- *"We're picking partners, not customers, for the first 50."*
