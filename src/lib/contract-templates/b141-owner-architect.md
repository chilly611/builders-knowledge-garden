## 6. Use of Documents (Instruments of Service)

Drawings, specifications, and other documents prepared by the Architect are Instruments of Service. The Architect retains copyright. The Owner is granted a non-exclusive license to use the Instruments of Service for the Project, conditioned on full payment of fees. The Owner may not reuse the documents on other projects without the Architect's written consent and additional compensation.

## 7. Professional Liability Insurance

The Architect shall maintain professional liability ("errors & omissions") insurance with limits of not less than $1,000,000 per claim and shall provide certificates of insurance on request.

## 8. Standard of Care

The Architect shall perform services consistent with the professional skill and care ordinarily provided by architects practicing in the same locality under the same or similar circumstances. The Architect does not warrant or guarantee the work of the contractor.

## 9. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement on seven (7) days' written notice if the other party materially breaches the Agreement and fails to cure within the notice period. On termination, the Owner shall pay for all services performed and reimbursable expenses incurred through the date of termination.

## 10. Dispute Resolution

The parties shall first attempt to resolve any dispute through direct good-faith discussion. If not resolved within thirty (30) days, the parties shall submit the dispute to mediation before pursuing litigation or arbitration. Each party bears its own mediation costs.

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California.

## 12. Entire Agreement

This Agreement is the entire agreement between the parties. It supersedes all prior discussions and writings and may be modified only in a signed writing.
