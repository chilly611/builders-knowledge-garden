## Design constraints
- Light backgrounds globally (warm-white #FAFAF8 cards on white #FFFFFF page; no dark surfaces ever)
- Archivo for body type
- Archivo Black for the hero line and "It's your home. You should see it being built."
- Killer App red #E8443A for the primary "Open [Project Name]" button
- Hero photo of the build above the salutation
- Footer link (small, gray, never bold) for privacy + data-sharing details

## ⚠ Legal review flags
- **"Approve pay applications and change orders"** — language sits at the intersection of UX clarity and e-sign legal validity (E-SIGN Act / state UETA). Counsel must confirm in-app tap constitutes binding signature, and confirm what disclosures must accompany the approval surface.
- **"Every dollar spent, committed, and remaining"** — implies completeness/accuracy. Confirm we are not creating an implied warranty of financial accuracy beyond what the platform can guarantee.
- **Footer "Privacy and data-sharing details" link** — destination must be the per-Lane data-sharing notice plus the platform privacy policy. CCPA applies for CA Owners (Marin demo).
